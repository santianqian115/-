Racing Tips 赛车技巧

  • Master the drift: Tap the brake while turning to initiate a drift, giving you better control through tight corners. 掌握漂移：转弯时轻点刹车开始漂移，让你在急转弯中获得更好的控制。
  • Find the racing line: The fastest path through any track isn't always the most obvious – experiment with different approaches to each corner. 寻找赛车线：通过任何赛道的最快路径并不总是最明显的——尝试不同的方式来处理每个转弯。
  • Look for shortcuts: Some tracks have hidden paths that can save precious seconds – but they often require precise driving to navigate successfully. 寻找捷径：有些赛道有隐藏的路径可以节省宝贵的时间——但它们通常需要精确驾驶才能成功通过。
  • Learn each vehicle: Every car handles differently – take time to understand the unique acceleration, top speed and grip characteristics of each one. 了解每辆车：每辆车的操控方式都不同——花时间了解每辆车独特的加速度、最高速度和抓地力特性。
  • Practice makes perfect: The difference between an average lap and a record-breaking one often comes down to millisecond-perfect timing and positioning. 熟能生巧：一般圈速与破纪录圈速之间的差异往往在于毫秒级的完美时机和位置。
