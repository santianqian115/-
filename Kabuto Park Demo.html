The Tiny Wonder of Kabuto Park 甲虫公园的微小奇迹

In a secluded corner of an ancient forest lies a miniature world invisible to human eyes — Kabuto Park, a thriving ecosystem where beetles are the size of people, leaves become massive canopies, and dewdrops transform into shimmering lakes. 在一片古老森林的隐秘角落，存在着一个人类肉眼看不见的微型世界——甲虫公园，一个繁荣的生态系统，在那里甲虫有人类大小，树叶变成巨大的顶棚，露珠化身为闪闪发光的湖泊。

You are a curious young beetle who has just earned their explorer's badge. Armed with nothing but your sense of wonder and a trusty magnifying glass, you set out to document the hidden treasures and untold stories of Kabuto Park's remarkable inhabitants. 你是一只刚获得探险家徽章的好奇小甲虫。除了好奇心和一个可靠的放大镜外一无所有，你开始记录甲虫公园非凡居民们的隐藏宝藏和未讲述的故事。

As you venture deeper into the park, you'll uncover the gentle rhythm of beetle society, help solve everyday problems for your six-legged neighbors, and perhaps even discover why strange shadows have been appearing at the edge of the forest lately... 当你深入公园探险时，你会发现甲虫社会的温和节奏，帮助你的六足邻居解决日常问题，甚至可能发现为什么最近森林边缘会出现奇怪的影子……

"The smallest leaf holds the greatest adventures, and the tiniest creatures tell the most magnificent stories." "最小的叶子蕴藏最伟大的冒险，最微小的生物讲述最壮观的故事。"
